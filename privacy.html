Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: We only collect your email address. We use it solely to notify you about flight delays and compensation. We never sell your data. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Who We Are

RyUnfair is a flight delay compensation tracking service operated by RyUnfair, registered in England and Wales.

Data Controller: RyUnfair

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

Data Purpose Legal Basis Retention
Email address Send delay notifications Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
Flight details (number, date, airports) Track delays and calculate compensation Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) Until you request deletion
Consent records Prove GDPR compliance Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) 7 years (UK legal requirement)

What We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your name (not required)
  • ❌ Your address (not required)
  • ❌ Payment information (handled by third-party if you donate)
  • ❌ Booking references or ticket numbers
  • ❌ Passport or ID information

3. How We Use Your Data

Your email address is used ONLY to:

  • Send you a verification email when you sign up
  • Notify you when your tracked flight qualifies for compensation
  • Send two follow-up emails (at 15 and 30 days) asking if we helped you claim
We will never: Sell your data, share it with airlines, send marketing emails (unless you explicitly opt in), or use your data for any purpose other than flight tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We share data with the following service providers (all GDPR compliant):

  • Supabase (EU region) - Database hosting
  • Resend - Email delivery
  • Vercel - Website hosting

We do NOT share your data with Ryanair, other airlines, or any marketing companies.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

Right to Access (Article 15)

You can request a copy of all data we hold about you.

→ Export your data

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You can request deletion of all your data at any time.

→ Delete your data

Right to Withdraw Consent

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in every email, or by contacting us.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Contact us to correct any inaccurate data.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can download your data in machine-readable JSON format.

Right to Complain

If you're unhappy with how we handle your data, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

6. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest (database encryption)
  • IP address hashing (we don't store your raw IP)
  • Row-level security in our database
  • Regular security audits

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to:

  • Remember your tracked flights locally (stays on your device)
  • Remember your cookie consent preference

We do NOT use tracking cookies, analytics, or any third-party cookies.

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data kept until you request deletion
  • After deletion request: Data anonymised within 24 hours, permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Audit logs: Kept for 7 years (UK legal requirement)

9. International Transfers

Your data is processed in the EU/UK. Our service providers (Supabase, Vercel, Resend) have data processing agreements ensuring GDPR compliance.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of any significant changes via email. Minor changes will be posted here with an updated date.
